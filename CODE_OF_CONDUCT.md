# Code Of Conduct

GridFleet should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate on device-lab infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially across different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep criticism focused on code, design, docs, tests, and operational impact.
- Make room for maintainers and contributors to ask clarifying questions.
- Be careful with security-sensitive details, private lab data, and credentials.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing exploit details or sensitive deployment information in public issues.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Pressuring maintainers for unpaid timelines, support guarantees, or private access.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues or pull requests, block users, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub. For vulnerabilities, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.
